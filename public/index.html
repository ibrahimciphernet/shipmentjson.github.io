Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common questions

You can track your package by entering your tracking number in the tracking section of our website. Tracking numbers are typically provided when your shipment is processed. If you don't have your tracking number, please contact the sender or check your email for shipping confirmation.

"In Transit" means your package is on its way to the destination. This status covers the time from when the package leaves the origin facility until it arrives at the destination facility. During this period, the package may be moving between different transportation hubs.

Tracking information may not update immediately due to several reasons:

  • The package hasn't been scanned at the next checkpoint
  • Technical delays in updating the tracking system
  • The package is in transit between facilities
  • International customs processing

If it's been more than 48 hours without an update, please contact our support team.

Our customer support team is available:

  • Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM (EST)
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM (EST)
  • Sunday: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (EST)

For urgent matters outside these hours, you can use our 24/7 emergency support line at +1 (800) 555-0199.

Address changes can be requested through your online account if the package hasn't reached the final delivery station. For packages already out for delivery, address changes may not be possible. Additional fees may apply for address changes. To request an address change:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Find the shipment in your dashboard
  3. Click "Change Delivery"
  4. Follow the prompts to enter the new address
